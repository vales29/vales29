# 💫 About Me:
Hi there! I’m a passionate Computer Engineering student at Goa College of Engineering specialising in frontend development. I love turning creative design concepts into clean, functional, and highly responsive web and mobile applications—whether that’s crafting interactive gamified e-learning platforms with Next.js or optimizing 3D workflows in Flutter. Having hands-on experience translating high-fidelity Figma wireframes into clean code, integrating real-time Firebase features, and driving frontend success in hackathons, my current focus is on expanding my engineering toolkit. Right now, I am dedicated to deep-diving into backend scalability with Node.js and Express.js, learning advanced state management, and exploring new architectural patterns to elevate my full-stack capabilities. Fun fact about me: I spent my school years in Bahrain before moving back to Goa for my engineering degree, so I bring a rich mix of global perspectives and cultural vibes into everything I build!
